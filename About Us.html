Not Only Her Qualifications Brings Her To Who She Is Today But Her Forteen (14) Years Experience As A Primary Teacher And Six (6) Years Experience In The Field Of Inclusive Education Molds Her To Be An EXPERT In The Field Of Inclusive Education.
